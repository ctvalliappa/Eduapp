Education Assistance

ANNOUNCEMENTS 
Application window for the 2018-2019 NSNA Education Scholarship will open on August 1st, 2018 and ends on September 30th, 2018.

NSNA's Education Assistance program provides scholarships to needy Nagarathar students to help with higher education expenses. Funds are generated through various fund raising programs in North America and through voluntary contributions of generous Nagarathars worldwide, both members and non-members of NSNA. Please note that interest from existing endowment funds are no longer sufficient to support educational assistance, so all donations are directly applied to assist individual students.

Please note that we do not provide scholarships to school going children at this time. We ONLY provide scholarships to college admitted candidates.

Scholarship Types

1. $300 (₹ 20,000 )

For a selected number of academically bright students, a merit scholarship in the amount of $300 per academic year is awarded. All applications go through a stringent review process including family income verification, transcripts, referral letters, and tuition fees. The number of awards is again limited and dependent on available funds

 

2. $150 (₹ 10,000)

This scholarship carry’s a stipend of $150 per academic year for any undergraduate program in India. Applicant needs to submit an application form every year and show proof of family income, mark sheets, tuition fees receipt and provide a referral letter from a reputed Nagarathar endorsing their eligibility. The number of awards each year is limited and dependent on the available funds
